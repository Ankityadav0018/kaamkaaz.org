Why Language Support Matters

Kaamkaaz is built for India's daily wage workers. Many workers are more comfortable in their native language rather than English. By expanding to regional languages, we ensure:

  • Better accessibility for workers in rural and semi-urban areas
  • Clearer communication between workers and recruiters
  • Faster job applications and hiring process
  • Inclusive platform for all Indian workers regardless of language preference
