About Kaamkaaz

Empowering daily wage workers and local recruiters through digital connectivity

Our Mission

"To bridge the gap between daily wage workers and hyperlocal job opportunities in rural and semi-urban India, ensuring dignity, transparency, and safety for everyone involved."

Our Story

Kaamkaaz was conceived out of a simple observation: millions of skilled daily wage earners (painters, plumbers, construction workers, agricultural laborers) in India waste hours every morning standing at local labor stands (labor chowks) hoping to find work. Simultaneously, local businesses and households struggle to find reliable workers on short notice.

Recognizing this massive inefficiency, we built Kaamkaaz to digitize the labor chowk. By leveraging GPS technology and simple mobile interfaces in local languages, we enable workers to apply for jobs from their homes and recruiters to hire verified workers instantly.

What Makes Us Different

  1. Hyperlocal Matching: Our GPS-enabled algorithm searches for jobs strictly within a 15-30km radius, minimizing travel time and cost.
  2. Aadhaar KYC Verification: Every worker undergoes secure Aadhaar and selfie verification to build trust and safety for recruiters and households.
  3. Direct Communication: No middlemen. Workers and recruiters can call, chat, or connect via WhatsApp directly through our interface.
  4. Completely Free Service: Completely Free Service: No registration or job posting charges. We believe in keeping core job search features free for all Indians.
  5. Local Language Support: Local Language Support: Full Hindi and English local translation switcher, making the platform accessible to everyone regardless of literacy barriers.

Our Core Values

Dignity & Respect

Treating every worker and labor seeker with the utmost respect and dignity they deserve.

Transparency

Fair wages, verified accounts, and clear communication guidelines across the board.

Safety First

Providing secure government identity checks to protect and secure our platform user community.

Accessibility

Building technologies that are simple, lightweight, offline-resilient, and multilingual.
