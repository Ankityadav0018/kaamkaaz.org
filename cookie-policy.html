1. Overview

Kaamkaaz uses cookies and similar technologies (like local storage) to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and improve our app. This policy explains what cookies we use and how you can control them.

We DO NOT use cookies for advertising, tracking, or selling your data.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that contain information about your interactions with Kaamkaaz. They help:

  • Keep you logged in securely
  • Remember your language preference
  • Cache job listings for offline access
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand how users interact with the app

3. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Token Keep you logged in securely. Validates your authentication. Until logout or 30 days
Language Preference Remember your chosen language (Hindi/English). 1 year
Location Preference Cache your location settings for faster job matching. 30 days
Job Cache Store downloaded job listings for offline access. Until cleared
App Version Track which app version you're using for compatibility. Until app update

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Kaamkaaz uses browser/app local storage to:

What's Stored Locally?

  • User Preferences: Language choice, theme, notifications settings
  • Offline Job Cache: Job listings downloaded for offline viewing
  • Search History: Recent job searches (optional, can be cleared)
  • Form Drafts: Unsaved job applications to prevent loss

Privacy:

All local storage is stored ON YOUR DEVICE ONLY. We cannot access it, and it is never sent to our servers unless you explicitly perform an action (like uploading an application).

5. Third-Party Services

Kaamkaaz uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

🔐 Firebase (Google)

  • Used for: Push notifications, analytics
  • Cookies set: Firebase tokens and session IDs
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

🔑 Supabase (Authentication)

  • Used for: User login, authentication
  • Cookies set: Auth tokens
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

No Advertising or Tracking Cookies

We DO NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising/tracking cookies. We DO NOT sell your data to advertisers.

6. How to Manage Cookies

Clear App Data / Cookies

You can clear cookies and app data anytime:

On Android:

  1. Go to Settings → Apps → Kaamkaaz
  2. Tap 'Storage'
  3. Tap 'Clear Cache' or 'Clear Data'

On Web Browser:

  1. Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
  2. Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
  3. Safari: Develop → Empty Caches

Disable Cookies in Browser

Most browsers allow you to disable cookies, but this may affect app functionality. You may not be able to stay logged in or access some features.

7. No Advertising Cookies

Kaamkaaz does NOT use advertising cookies or tracking pixels for marketing purposes.

We do NOT:

  • Use Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • Track you across other websites
  • Sell your browsing data or behavior to advertisers
  • Use cookies for targeted advertising
  • Share your data with ad networks

Your privacy is important to us.
