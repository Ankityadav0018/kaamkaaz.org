4. KYC & Verification Requirements

For Workers:

To build trust and reduce fraud, workers must complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification:

  • Aadhaar Number - Government-issued unique identifier
  • Live Selfie - For facial recognition and liveness verification
  • Driving License (Optional) - Additional identity proof (optional)

KYC Process:

  1. Submit Aadhaar number and associated details
  2. Take a live selfie for face verification
  3. Upload optional documents (Driving License)
  4. Wait for admin verification (typically 24-48 hours)
  5. Once approved, you get a "KYC Verified" badge

Important:

  • KYC verification is mandatory to apply for jobs
  • False or fraudulent KYC information will result in permanent account termination
  • Your KYC documents are stored securely and NOT shared with recruiters
  • Only a "Verified" badge is visible to recruiters for privacy

For Recruiters:

Recruiters must verify their contact information and company details:

  • Phone number verification (OTP confirmation)
  • Email verification
  • Company/Organization information (if applicable)

5. Recruiter Responsibilities

Legal Compliance:

  • Post only legal, legitimate job opportunities
  • Comply with all applicable labor laws, including:
    • Minimum Wages Act
    • Payment of Wages Act
    • Occupational Safety, Health & Working Conditions Code, 2020
    • Child Labor Prohibition & Regulation Act
  • Do not post jobs that violate Indian law or involve illegal activities

Job Posting Standards:

  • Accurate Information: Job title, location, pay rate, duration, responsibilities must be truthful
  • Fair Wages: Compensation must meet or exceed applicable minimum wage laws
  • Safety Standards: Ensure jobs meet occupational health and safety requirements
  • No Discrimination: Do not discriminate based on caste, religion, gender, age, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Clear Terms: Be clear about work hours, conditions, equipment, hazards, and payment terms

Worker Treatment:

  • Pay workers agreed wages on time
  • Provide safe working conditions
  • No harassment, abuse, or unfair treatment
  • Respect worker privacy and dignity
  • Do not ask for payment or bribes from workers

Communication Standards:

  • Respond to worker inquiries promptly and professionally
  • Be clear about job expectations and timelines
  • Do not use offensive, abusive, or harassing language
  • Respect worker preferences for contact methods (chat, call, WhatsApp)

Account Responsibilities:

  • Do not post duplicate or spam job listings
  • Do not impersonate other companies or individuals
  • Do not share workers' contact information with third parties without consent
  • Do not misuse workers' data for purposes other than hiring
