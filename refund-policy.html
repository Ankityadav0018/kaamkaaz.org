1. Current Fees: ₹29 Posting Fee

Kaamkaaz is completely FREE for Workers. Recruiters pay a small posting fee of ₹29 per job listing.

Most features of Kaamkaaz remain free:

  • ✅ Create a worker or recruiter account — FREE
  • ✅ Search and apply for jobs — FREE
  • ✅ Access KYC verification — FREE
  • ✅ Use in-app chat — FREE
  • ✅ View job location and details — FREE

Recruiters pay a small one-time fee of ₹29 to publish a job. This fee is generally non-refundable once the job is successfully published.

2. Future Paid Features Disclaimer

Kaamkaaz may introduce optional paid features in the future, such as:

  • Premium job posting (featured listings)
  • Advanced recruiter tools (analytics, bulk messaging)
  • Worker profile highlights
  • Priority job matching
  • Other value-added services

Important Notice:

If and when paid features are introduced:

  • Core features will remain FREE for all users
  • Users will receive 30 days advance notice
  • This Refund Policy will be updated with specific terms
  • Opt-in only — you choose whether to upgrade
  • You will have the right to cancel anytime

Once paid features are introduced, this policy will be updated with detailed refund terms for those features.

3. General Refund Principles

If and when Kaamkaaz introduces paid features, the following refund principles will apply:

Refund Eligibility:

  • Time Window: Refund requests must be made within 7 days of payment
  • Service Delivery: No refund if the service has already been delivered or used
  • Fraud Prevention: No refund if the request is fraudulent or policy-violating

Refund Processing:

  • Timeline: Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original Method: Refunds issued via the original payment method only
  • Bank Processing: Your bank may take additional 2-3 days to credit your account

Non-Refundable Items:

  • Services already delivered or completed
  • Feature access that was already used
  • Payments made more than 7 days ago
  • Fraudulent or duplicate payments

Refund Request Status:

  • You will receive email confirmation of your refund request
  • Approval/rejection decision will be communicated within 5 business days
  • You can check refund status in your account settings

4. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: In-App Request (Recommended)

  • Open Kaamkaaz app → Profile → Settings → Request Refund
  • Select the payment/feature for which you want a refund
  • Provide a reason for the refund request
  • Submit the request
  • Confirmation email will be sent within 24 hours

Step 2: Email Request

If in-app request is not available, email us at:

Email: kaamkaaz0018@gmail.com

Subject Line: Refund Request - [Your Phone Number] - [Date of Payment]

Email Content Should Include:

  • Your full name and registered phone number
  • Date of payment
  • Feature/service purchased
  • Amount paid
  • Reason for refund request
  • Payment method used (credit card, debit card, UPI, etc.)

Thank you.

Response Timeline:

  • Initial review: 1-2 business days
  • Decision communicated: 5 business days
  • Refund processing: 5-7 business days (if approved)

5. Google Play Store Billing

Google Play Refund Policy:

If you purchased any Kaamkaaz premium features or in-app items through Google Play Store, you are also covered by Google's refund policy:

  • Policy: Google Play offers a 48-hour refund window for most purchases
  • How to Request: Open Google Play Store → My Apps & Games → Installed → Kaamkaaz → Tap menu → Report a Problem
  • More Info: Google Play Billing Help

Important Note:

You can request a refund through either:

  • Option 1: Google Play's 48-hour refund window (fastest)
  • Option 2: Kaamkaaz's 7-day refund request (if Google Play period has passed)

We recommend using Google Play's refund option first as it is processed immediately.

Payment Methods via Google Play:

  • Google Play Credit
  • Credit/Debit Card linked to Google Play
  • UPI (in India)
  • Carrier billing (if enabled)
