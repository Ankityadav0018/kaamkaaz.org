Brand Overview

About Kaamkaaz

Kaamkaaz is a hyperlocal mobile platform that leverages GPS technology and multi-language support to directly connect daily wage workers with local recruiters in India. By eliminating middleman commissions and providing government-backed Aadhaar KYC trust markers, Kaamkaaz provides a digital, transparent, and fair framework to support India's informal labor workforce.

Key Facts

  • Developer: Ankit Yadav (Individual Developer)
  • Launched Date: May 2025
  • Headquarters Location: Rajasthan, India
  • Pricing Structure: Free for workers, ₹9 posting fee for recruiters
  • Target Market: Rural and semi-urban districts across India
