**Kaamkaaz: Connecting Bharat to Local Jobs & Skilled Workers**

Kaamkaaz is India's premier hyperlocal marketplace designed specifically for daily wage workers and local recruiters. Whether you are a skilled professional looking for work or a household/business in need of immediate help, Kaamkaaz bridges the gap with a simple, secure, and smart mobile experience.

### For Workers:
Find jobs that match your skills right in your neighborhood. From plumbing and electrical work to construction and cleaning, Kaamkaaz brings the work to you.
- **Smart Job Discovery:** Automatically see jobs within a 50km radius, smartly sorted from nearest to farthest. Want to travel further? Easily expand your search!
- **Skill Badges:** Get your skills verified by the Kaamkaaz team to stand out and earn more.
- **Referral Rewards:** Refer your friends and earn ₹20 for every successful registration.
- **Multilingual Support:** Available in 12 Indian languages including Hindi, English, Rajasthani, Haryanvi, Punjabi, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, and Gujarati.
- **Offline Mode:** Access your applied jobs and recruiter details even without an active internet connection.

### For Recruiters:
Hire verified, reliable workers for your home, farm, factory, or office in minutes.
- **KYC Verified Workers:** View Aadhaar-verified profiles with real-time ratings and job history.
- **In-App Chat:** Communicate directly with workers to discuss wages and timing.
- **Secure Hiring:** Contact details are only revealed after you officially hire a worker, protecting your privacy.
- **Small Posting Fee:** Publish a job for just ₹9—no hidden commissions or high agency costs.

### Key Features:
- **Real-Time Notifications:** Get instant alerts for new jobs or applicant updates.
- **Transparent Ratings:** Build your reputation with a 5-star rating system.
- **Support for All Roles:** From Plumbers and Electricians to Cooks, Drivers, and Masonry (Mistri).
- **Safe & Secure:** Robust data protection and Aadhaar-based verification for peace of mind.
